Analytics of the sites published by the company

#P2Enjoy use Umami for its analytics on theirs published websites.

Umami does not collect any personally identifiable information and all data collected is anonymous. Users cannot be identified and are never tracked across websites. All data is stored as aggregated statistics only.

The statistics being aggregated and stored are:

  • counters for each visited pages
  • counters for visitors page referrers
  • counter for name and version of the browser of the visitors used to navigate the site
  • counter for name and version of the operating system of the visitors
  • counters for each generic class of the device being used by the visitor like desktop pc, mobile phone, tablet...
  • counters for the country of the visitors: no city, no region, no street... only the country.

Statistical data is retained for as long as the referred service is available on-line and it's deleted once the service target of the analytics goes off-line.
