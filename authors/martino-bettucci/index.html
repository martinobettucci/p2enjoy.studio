Martino BETTUCCI (28)

He was born by the year 1984. Passionate about Sci-Fi, he like to read Philip Kindred Dick, Stephen King, George Orwell and Isaac Asimov. He always wanted to go for engineering and possibly be the author of the next big thing or create a time-machine and was fascinated by technologies as soon as possible during his youth. He spends most of his free time writing software and custom hardware who God's knows what they meant to be and occasionally figure out a useful way to exploit them so he likes to share them for free. He firmly believes that knowledge and software should be free and accessible for everyone to accelerate progress and individual growth. As such, he does only use free software for all his personals AND professionals works. When he does not teach "Agile Methods" or "Innovation Thinking" in universities all around the EU, he likes to draw pencil and painting big robots, play paper's RPG with his friends or train hard in martial arts.
