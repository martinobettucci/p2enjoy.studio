History has begun

 

Established in 2022, "P2Enjoy" was created by a mad scientists : ⚛Martino BETTUCCI🧪

🗓 Planning to spread the knowledge and education on emerging technologies to the newest generations 💻 (he is a teacher 👨‍🏫 after all), he started to thinker with Solidity 📜 and JavaScript ☕ and game development 🚀; writing and sharing technical 👨‍💻 👩‍💻 on all existing socials in order to reach as much as possible of enthusiasts 🎉 like him.

Along the journey 🚘, some have become like friends and he was joined by these fearless explorers of the block-chain universe; So they decided to go for a team. 👥

Today #P2Enjoy is a company run by a bold CEO working with 8+ skilled freelancers on a per mission basis. 🥰

And we want to help the future generations to build a better block-chain ecosystem for the developers, the users and for the planet 🍃. 

Who work with us? 💼

Investors, accountants, lawyers, game designers, composers, illustrators, community managers, developers and many more to come along the way forward!
